Description

Our mission is to develop open source solutions and provides professional support helps small and medium size companies meet the challenges of developing professional Arabic websites in the PHP/MySQL environment based on our experience in Arabic language processing, the library that we develop helps companies save time and increase productivity.

Ar-PHP Web Site

Categories

CGI Tools/Libraries

License

Features

  • Advanced Arabic search (stem based)
  • Render Arabic text (PDF, GD, SWF)
  • Present dates in Arabic or Hijri and convert Hijri date into Unix timestamp
  • Transliterate English words in Arabic and vice versa
  • Spell numbers in Arabic idiom
  • Arabic text auto summarization
  • Phonetically alike Arabic words (soundex)
  • Guess gender of Arabic names
  • Calculate the time of Muslim prayer
  • Standardize Arabic text and text normalisation
  • and many more ...
